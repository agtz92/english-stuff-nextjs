  ### **Step 1: Disassemble the Pegs**


  ![DIY Wooden Peg Snowflake](https://www.bosch-diy.com/images/disassemble-clothspin-fir-cone-bosch-diy--d64d0a939a084f93891143bd7ecf4e52.jpg?imgWidth=750&imgHeight=421&scale=1 "DIY Wooden Peg Snowflake")


  **Prepare the wooden pegs for assembly**


  1. Remove the metal springs from the pegs by hand.

  2. Separate each peg into two wooden pieces.


  <!--EndFragment-->
mk3: >-
  <!--StartFragment-->


  ### **Step 2: Glue the Peg Halves Together**


  ![DIY Wooden Peg Snowflake](https://www.bosch-diy.com/images/connecting-clothespin-halfs-gluepen-bosch-diy--298e2d7a195c42feb453a52d638c12ee.jpg?imgWidth=750&imgHeight=421&scale=1 "DIY Wooden Peg Snowflake")


  **Form pairs of wooden peg halves**


  1. Apply glue to the flat backs of the separated wooden pieces.

  2. Press two halves together, ensuring a firm bond.


  <!--EndFragment-->
mk4: >-
  <!--StartFragment-->


  ### **Step 3: Assemble the Snowflake Shape**


  ![DIY Wooden Peg Snowflake](https://www.bosch-diy.com/images/gluepen-glueing-clothspins-circle-bosch-diy--170073d367004a2992af7d8553d29536.jpg?imgWidth=750&imgHeight=421&scale=1 "DIY Wooden Peg Snowflake")


  **Create the iconic snowflake pattern**


  1. Take the glued peg pairs and apply glue to the pointed ends.

  2. Attach the pairs together to form a circular snowflake shape.

  3. Repeat until the entire circle is complete, pressing firmly to secure the structure.


  <!--EndFragment-->

  ### **Conclusion**


  Your wooden snowflake is now ready to decorate your home! Hang it with a string or wire, or place it on a table as a charming centerpiece. Looking for more ideas? Try making a Christmas star or walnut wreath for even more festive fun.
